Haunted Mansion

CREATED: October 2006.
CREATOR: Shedletsky.
PERMISSION: UNCOPYLOCKED
STATUS: PUBLIC - BROKEN
GAMEMODE: Free For All.

TRIVIA

  • This game is one of the first games to show festive toolbox skins.

  • This map appeared in the 2006 ROBLOX trailer.

  • The ghost icon used for the jet boots re-skin is taken from a Uttrevia.com Halloween trivia page from 2007 visit it here.

    - Shedletsky.

    INTRODUCTION

    Haunted Mansion is a brickbattle map created by Shedletsky to celebrate Halloween in 2006, the game features a mansion with multiple rooms, and an underground cave, the game was released under the ROBLOX account, it used the classic brickbattle toolset with the particularity that they where changed to a more spooky-themed look.
    The game is broken, but is playable under Shedletsky's account.

    HISTORY

    During the Halloween of 2006, Shedletsky created and released the game under ROBLOX's profile, the mansion itself haves a variety of rooms to explore, outside the mansion, there is a pumpkin farm with a scarecrow, a lake, and a playground behind the castle.

    Inside The Mansion

    The entrance room haves a hole that leads to the underground cave, theres a restroom with furnitures and a fireplace, theres also a room with a dinner table, a kitchen with a fridge with heads int it, the stairs lead to the second floor, with a balcony, two beds and a room with a bed and a cabinet.

    Theres another entrance to the mansion in the left, which leads to the Red room, the stairs in the red room will lead to the underground cave, thought most part of it is behind a small window, theres a fake wall that hides the Witches room, inside, theres a pot, the liquid on it will make the player's head bigger and will change it to a random color, it will kill upon contact, there are also a desk with papers and a cabinet with heads of different sizes, however, nowadays, it is broken, it will give the effects on the head of the player but will not kill them.

    the balcony haves a ledge that leads to the ceiling, and outside the mansion, there is a mountain with a hole that leads to the basement cave, in the cave, there are pillars and stairs, there are also 2 different doors, one leads to the playground and one leads to the lake.

    Halloween toolset

    The players are given the move tool, a rocket launcher, slinghost and bomb, the last three mentioned have went trought a re-skin to match the Halloween ambience, the rocket launcher's rockets are organge, the slinghosts are replaced with heads, and the bomb is replaced with a pumpkin, there is also a teleporter that sends you to the mountain with the pit, making you fall to the underground cave, there is also the Jet Boots, that have been reskinned to a ghost effect. As today, the game is broken, and the weapons dont work, however the game is public in Shedletsky's account and its uncopylocked.
