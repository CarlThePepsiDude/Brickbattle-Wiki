Grand Melee Arena

CREATED: August 27, 2007.
CREATOR: Shedletsky.
PERMISSION: UNCOPYLOCKED
STATUS: PUBLIC - BROKEN
GAMEMODE: Free For All.

TRIVIA

  • This arena has been re-used in another game by Shedletsky called "Sword Training".

  • The map's structure has been breaking trought time.

    • "After a very tough competition between some of the best bloxxers in Robloxia, four titans have emerged victorious! These four battled their way to the final round through hundreds of other competitors."

    INTRODUCTION

    The Grand Melee Arena is a colosseum made by Shedletsky for the Roblox Grand Melee tournament, it was used for the final round of the tournament, the main arena is closed with invisible insta-kill walls to prevent non-finalist players to interrupt, there where messages at the top (hints), announcing the final round and advicing that incoming spectators could be kicked from the game if the limit of players was reached.

    The game is now broken, but is uncopylocked and can also be played online.

    HISTORY

    The map was made and hosted in the "GrandMelee" account as well as the past two rounds, The colosseum is conformed of an entrance with stairs, and the Grand Melee logo at the sides, inside, there is the main arena where only the "Gladiators" team were allowed, team which was conformed of the finalist of the tournament, those on the Spectators team could only watch, any attempt to jump into the arena would be greated by getting bloxxed by invisble insta-kill walls protecting the arena, the finnalist would spawn inside the arena in any of the 16 spawns placed around the arena, as well as respawn, there where also 4 Grand Melee logos in the invisible walls in each side of the arena.

    For the spectators, there where stairs that leads to the second and third floors, where they can see the fight, there is also another entrance via stairs that leads to a basement with a door where you could access the arena, but it was closed with a wall for already mentioned reasons.

    the whole colosseum is placed in a green baseplate and a gray road for the entrance.

    As of now, the map is public and can be played, but its broken, all of the brickbattle classic weapons are used except for the paintballgun, there are not much recreations of the Grand Melee as a stand alone map, but it can be seen in other games.

    Since the Grand Melee Arena has been uncopylocked since its release during the tournament, users would reupload it and change the scripts containing the users allowed in the arena, so they could play the map properly with friends.
