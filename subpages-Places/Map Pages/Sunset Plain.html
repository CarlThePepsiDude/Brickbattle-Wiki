Sunset Plain

CREATED: November 15, 2006.
CREATOR: Koopa.
PERMISSION: UNCOPYLOCKED
STATUS: PUBLIC - BROKEN
GAMEMODE: Free For All.

TRIVIA

  • This is one of the first brickbattle games.

  • This map appeared in the 2006 ROBLOX trailer.

    • "Have fun at Sunset Plain! unfortunatly i could not get the regenerate scripts to work in this map so nothing regenerates obviosly. You might have already seen some of this map from the adventure awaits movie on the homepage of roblox. So have fun here! P.S check out the waterfall!"

    - Schwaabo.

    INTRODUCTION

    Sunset Plain is a brickbattle game made by Koopa, and hosted in his alt Schwaabo, it is one of the most remembered classic maps in ROBLOX, and has been featured in a post in the ROBLOX blog, as of today the game is broken, but can still played and its uncopylocked for the players to fix it themselves.
    The game is now hosted in the ROBLOX account, its broken, and it has shut down, but it is uncopylocked.

    HISTORY

    Back in the early days of ROBLOX, Sunset Plains was made and even featured on the 2006 ROBLOX trailer, since Koopa couldn't get the regen scripts to work, the map will not regenerate and will stay destroyes until everyone on the server leaves and a new one is created.

    This map contains two towers, one of them is a watchtower, a mountain, a waterfall with a cave behind it, a Dog swamp, and a castle in the center.

    As of now, the map is public and can be played, but its broken, there are no weapons, and the only thing players can do is explore the map.
