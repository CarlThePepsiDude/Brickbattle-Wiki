Glass Houses

CREATED: February 16, 2007
CREATOR: ROBLOX.
PERMISSION: UNCOPYLOCKED
STATUS: PRIVATED - BROKEN
GAMEMODE: Team Deathmatch

TRIVIA

  • This is probaly the first team-based brickbattle map, Crossroads was supposed to take that place, but back then, teams wheren't added in studio and team beacons where the only thing that could made it work.

    • "Battle it out with friends in this classic destructible environment!"

    - Glass Houses current description.

    INTRODUCTION

    Glass Houses is a Team-Deathmatch brickbattle map by roblox, this map is focused on leading your team trought victory by having more KO's than the other team, with the help of killbots.

    HISTORY

    This map was created on 2007 and hosted in the ROBLOX account, it received a lot of players, unfortunately, like the other games in ROBLOX account, it broke due to updates and finally got privated, but also uncopylocked so players can fix it and reupload it.

    The map haves 2 bases, each base is for a team, the bases includes cannons, which can be fired throught buttons, and can also be moved to the desired position to shoot, cannons shoot 4 to 6 and killbot orbs where players can spawn a killbot that will help theyr team, there is also a tower in the middle, where a yellow killbot spawns, this one will attack every player regardless of theyr team, the killbots can be destroyed.

    The teams must fight for a time of 3 minutes, when the time runs out, the game shows which team has won, and the map resets.
