    INTRODUCTION

    Balance is a map created by Matt Dussek, is one of the most remembered classic brickbattle games, even being recreated in Super Nostalgia Zone, the game is known for taking place in an orange baseplate that will flip depending on how much weight it haves.
    The game is public under Matt's account, but is broken, not only the weapons but the baseplate will not move either, however it is uncopylocked.

    HISTORY

    In March 2008, Matt Dussek released his game "Balance", this map only featured a lone orange baseplate, with 4 spawns at each corner, the baseplate would flip depending on the height and players will have to fight to not fall while bloxxing other players, the only weapons present on the game are the trowel and the superball, back then, the superball used to fling players when it hit an exact spot of a player, in the map, being flung is certain dead.

    To keep the balance of the baseplate, shooting superballs at the ground may work in the correct moment, as well as placing trowels, however you need to be precise or the trowel may crumble due to the baseplate being inclined, it is also recomended to stay in ledges in case you have to jump to the other face of the baseplate if it flips entirely.

    as of today, the game is broken, but can be fixed and reuploaded by other players.
