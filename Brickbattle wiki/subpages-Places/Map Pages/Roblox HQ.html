Roblox World Headquarters

CREATED: November, 2006.
CREATOR: Shedletsky.
PERMISSION: UNCOPYLOCKED
STATUS: PRIVATED - BROKEN
GAMEMODE: Free For All.

TRIVIA

  • It used to be Builderman's starter place.

  • This map appeared in the 2006 ROBLOX trailer.

  • This map haves a second version called "Roblox HQ 2.0".

  • There's an early version of the map that only appeared in the 2006 ROBLOX trailer, it used to have only one tower instead of two.

    INTRODUCTION

    Roblox World Headquarters, or Roblox HQ for short, is a map created by Shedletsky and hosted in Builderman's account, the main building is conformed by two towers, connected by a bridge, the left-tower haves an R in the front face, inside the towers there are placed dummys of the ROBLOX admins, there are other structures in the map, a tower in ruins at the corner of a lake, and taken from the Beta versions of Crossroads, the medical house and a castle.
    to this day, the game has been privated, due to the toolset breaking, the game can still be opened trought studio.

    HISTORY

    This place used to be builderman's starter place until Shedletsky updated the game by creating the map we know today, the map features two buildings, with ROBLOX admins inside them, in the left-tower, Matt Duseck and Stoecker are in the basement, and in the office at second floor, Erik Cassel is on the seat, and next to him is Telamon(Shedletsky). In the right-tower, Clockwork and Wookong are in the basement, and Builderman is sitting in the office

    The map is known for having more than 5.000 bricks, the more the towers are destroyed, more lag it will cause, and since there are no regen scripts, the game becomes unplayable at that point, the players are given the classic brickbattle toolset with the addition of the jet boots.

    The game can only be played in multyplayer via recreations, in that case, brickbattlers often fight outside the towers to prevent the game from lagging, while sometimes people only join to fest on the thousands of bricks at 16 fps, most of the videos about Roblox HQ are about it being completely nuked in high speed to hide the low fps and to give a more epic looking of the destruction of the towers.
