    DESCRIPTION

    M1LKMAN is one of the oldest brickbattlers and users on ROBLOX, joining in 2007. he is recognized for achieving 100,000 K.Os before any other user on the platform with a 2:1 ratio. He also used to be an active Crossroads player, as he stated in an interview. M1LKMAN is remembered as one of the best Brickbattlers of his era. Although M1LKMAN rarely logs onto ROBLOX anymore, but he stills check the platform time to time.

    JOURNEY

    since M1LKMAN created his account on ROBLOX, the only thing he played was Crossroads, he quickly began to be known among the recurrent Crossroads players back in the day as he would be normally seen pwning people in there, after aproximately two years, he reached 100K K.Os before any other ROBLOX user could, achieving the title of the K.O King, without exploits nor camping. He later joined at the group Black Bloxxers.
