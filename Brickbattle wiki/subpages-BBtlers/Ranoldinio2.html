    DESCRIPTION

    ranoldinio2, or simply Ran as his friends call him, is a very well-known and skilled Brickbattler with many years of activity. He still holds that title to this day. Ran has been a consistent participant in the Black Flame tournament series, earning the title of 'Black Iron Champion' in both BF1 and BF2. Ranoldinio is the founder of the group "Brickbattle Bloxxers", which is affiliated with T.O.B.

    JOURNEY

    Ranoldinio2 started Brickbattling in 2011. By 2013, Ran became a prominent player, especially after defeating Tyler9698. Since then, his journey of improvement as a Brickbattler began, winning the first two Black Flames organized by Koke15 and gradually becoming more recognizable. To this day, he remains a cheerleader for the community and is still active.
