    DESCRIPTION

    Thegameboy is a former brickbattler who is known for his massive contributions to the community in developing games and scripts. He developed most of the Colosseum, and along with koke later created the New Colosseum, He also was a major contributor in the Ultimate Toolset.
    He is no longer active in the community, apparently due to toxicity, but he is remembered as one of the biggest developers in the community.

    JOURNEY

    Gameboy joined the community trought Doomspire Brickbattle recently after the events of the Newgens vs Veterans War in 2019. He then would begin working on projects like his own toolset and a Colosseum, which worked for duels, and worked for for Black Flame duels. Later he would develop The new Colosseum along side koke, which also served for Black flame tournaments.
