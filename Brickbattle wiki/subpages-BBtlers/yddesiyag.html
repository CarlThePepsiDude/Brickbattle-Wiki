    DESCRIPTION

    yddesiyag is a former skilled brickbattler that was active from 2019 to 2022, known for his skill, and the tournaments he has been champion of, he's also one of the only skilled ipad brickbattlers.

    JOURNEY

    yddesiyag was a Crossroads player back in 2014, going under the name of "thefurrycheetah3d", at a very young age, meeting "Madsanity" during his frequent times playing the game, he would later stop playing the game after a year.

    He would begin brickbattling around August 2019, after stumbling across Doomspire Brickbattle, and revisiting Crossroads, an user by the name "EarlDeer" asked yddesiyag to duel in gameboy's private Crossroads, where EarlDeer would defeat yddesiyag 7 - 0, the duel triggered cheetah's interest in brickbattling again.

    He then would lurk in Crossroads or Junkkid's private Crossroads, meeting more brickbattlers, in less of 3 weeks, Thegameboy did a private ranktest to cheetah, that finished with cheetah earning the Warrior rank.

    yddesiyag made contact with an inner community when an user called "NewtonVolta9" invited him to the group Covenant of Brickbattlers.

    In 2020, yddesiyag would earn the Master rank after defeating other Masters and being ranktested by Sockjoke during the Ballerroads Tournament, and later would win the first 2v2 tournament along with Boy4u2. and during Late 2022, he was awarded with the Grandmaster rank after winning the BBC Tournament.
