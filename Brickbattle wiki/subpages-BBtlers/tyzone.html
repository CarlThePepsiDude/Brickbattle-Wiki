    DESCRIPTION

    Tyzone is a french developer that used to brickbattle, he is known for his projects for the brickbattle community, most known works of his are Brickbattle Frenzy, and his involvement in the Ultimate Toolset, he is currently inactive in the competitive side of the community, but he sometimes keeps working on his projects that involves brickbattling.

    JOURNEY

    tyzone didn't instantly joined the community, around 2008 - 2009, he was a constant Crossroads player, without being aware of an inner brickbattling community, therefore, he learned experience for the future, it wasn't until January 2020, where thanks to a video talking about modern brickbattle, he became interested enought to make the first contact with the community, after the video, he played in Chaos Canyon and met "accurative", after a duel, he got invested into high-level brickbattle, now being more active on Crossroads.

    After some time, he would met members of the Covenant Of Brickbattlers group and was later invited into the group in May of the same year.

    As of some time between May and April of 2020, he earned the Knight rank on his first ranktest, and when Black FLame 4 began, he achieved the Centurion rank.

    Tyzone's works

    While interested in competition, tyzone began working on projects that would later make him known, models like a tool visualizer, a custom gravity Crossroads, a practice game, and the brickbattle timeline project.

    Editor's note: The last game is the inspiration for this wiki!! :D

    His most memorable works are helping in the Ultimate Toolset, the spectator mode used in most of the current brickbattle games, and his game "Brickbattle Frenzy", where he worked on training bots that have a good ai, they can attack other players, bomb jump, and follow each gamemode objetive.

    Before going inactive in the community, he wanted to revamp the ultimate toolset into something that had every performance improvement, but due to it not being as much of a chore to edit, it never became a thing.

    tyzone is remembered as one of the users who most offered content for the community.
