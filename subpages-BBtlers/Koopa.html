    DESCRIPTION

    Koopa, or Schwaabo is a veretan ROBLOX user that is known for the prices of many events he holds, Koopa is mostly known between the brickbattling community for being the winner of the Roblox Grand Melee, He is also the creator of Sunset Plain, a classic brickbattle map his alt "Schwaabo" hosts to this day

    JOURNEY

    Koopa is known for holding many early era events prices, including the Black Iron Crown of Pwnage, and the Kleos Aphthiton, He mostly used his alt Schwaabo to post youtube videos, however, his channel seems to not have shown any sign of life in more than a decade, since there are only 4 videos uploaded dating from 2007. He has created many models and packages, and became a forum moderator in November 14th, 2007. He is currently inactive and only checks his profile after some time.
