    DESCRIPTION

    Koke15 is a Mexican oldgen brickbattler, he's current leader of the "Temple Of Brickbattle" group, he is known for his projects, works and his tournaments, He is seen as a nice guy between the community.
    Koke is known for his tournaments, the most known is "Black Flame", an on-going brickbattle tournament series that occurs every year, however, Brickbattlers waited 3 years for the sixth Black Flame to happen until Koke finally announced it on Mid-2024.
    He is currently working on updates for Doomspire Brickbattle and more events for T.O.B.

    JOURNEY

    Koke began brickbattling in the winter of 2010. His begginings involved the goal of joining Prymed, the main hub at the time, however, he got rejected a few times due to being a newbie until he achieved the needed skill to join, later, he was also welcomed into Brick Kings.

    Eventually, the demise of Brick Kings made Koke to go inactive, returning in 2016 where a lack of brickbattlers was noticeable, creating "Order Of Brickbattle". In late 2017, He assisted in the creation of "Temple Of Brickbattle", becoming a rankmaster in the group, gaining recognition as a community leader.

    Additionally, he created "Black Flame", an ongoing series of tournaments based on the Roblox Grand Melee, and one of the most important tournaments on modern brickbattle... the Black Flame has now reached its sixth iteration in 2024.

    After the incident surrounding GFink's expulsion, ownership of T.O.B. was granted to Koke, due to his good leadership and ideas.
