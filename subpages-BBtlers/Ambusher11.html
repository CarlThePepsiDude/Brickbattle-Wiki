    DESCRIPTION

    Ambusher11, currently known as PunishedAmbusher is a veteran brickbattler that began brickbattling in 2009 and became less active in later 2010's.
    He is known for being one of the participants in the Roblox Grand Melee, and is one, if not the only, oldest brickbattlers that remain active until today (though not very active right now), in terms of join date.
    Ambusher barely logs into his account, but he joins time-to-time and sometimes plays on TOBs events.

    JOURNEY

    Ambusher11 began brickbattling in 2009 and eventually gained recognition in the brickbattle community, he is not very active right now but he still interacts with other brickbattlers.

    Unfair termination

    In 2020, Ambusher's account was falsely terminated. An user targeted Ambusher and submitted a support ticket written in all caps and filled with curses, requesting the termination of Ambusher's account. Unfortunately, the ticket was approved, resulting in the termination of Ambusher11's account for being 'compromised.' Appeals were denied, with the support team stating that he was 'not the owner of the account.' Ambusher shared this incident on Roblox's subreddit, but the post was later removed by subreddit moderators due to a rule violation. While he remained active on the platform, his original 12-year-old account (at the time) was permanently lost. However, he returned under his new account, PunishedAmbusher, which he continues to use to this day. The subreddit post's comment section was filled with replies from users expressing their disappointment with Roblox for approving such a poorly made ticket. This incident is remembered as one of the most unfair decisions by Roblox staff.

    The responsible for Ambusher's ban was Ziraxiam, a problematic user who had understanding on how reports worked, and could use that to his advantage, however, sometime after what happened, Ziraxiam left the internet, due to being targeted and constantly attacked by the community for everything he has done.

    Ambusher is one if not the only ROBLOX veteran that is still around in the brickbattle community, he doesnt play Roblox much, but he is still in contact and interacts with it frequently.
