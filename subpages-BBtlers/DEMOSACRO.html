    DESCRIPTION

    Demo, or Flame, is a Top-Brickbattler that has won multiple brickbattle tournaments during his journey, the most known one being Black Flame 5.

    JOURNEY

    DEMOSACRO joined the brickbattle community in 2018, he soon became and skilled brickbattler, winning tournaments like Black Flame 5, was the finalist of Black Flame 3 and 4, he won GFink's 30k Robux tournament, along side Frostelther won the Sactun 2v2 Tournament, and currently he has won abraxas tournament (2025).

    He obtained the Master rank on September 28th 2019, and the title of grandmaster on January 15th 2021, later when Koke took TOB's leadership and ranks where rebranded, DEMOSACRO obtained the Legend rank.
